The idea is that the probability of you dropping a pin on a series of vertical lines and having it interect is PI. The length of the pin is 2/3 the distance between the vertical lines. So click away at the canvs and see what the probability is!

The idea can be applied to several real world problems such as traffic, purchases, and telecommunications.
